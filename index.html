How Can You Help to Fight Climate Change?

Team Trees

A collaborative fundraiser that raised 20 million U.S. dollars before 2020 to plant 20 million trees. The initiative was started by American YouTubers MrBeast and Mark Rober

Team Seas

An international collaborative fundraiser project run by YouTubers MrBeast and Mark Rober. They aim to raise 30 million dollars, which would allow them to remove 30,000,000 pounds of marine debris from the ocean by the end of 2021.

Hold Big Business Accountable

Just 100 companies responsible for 71% of global emissions. Climate change is not a personal problem and it need solutions that limit corporate emitters.

Talk to Your Repersentives

Make calls, write letters, attend town halls. What you can help your leaders understand is that it’s just as important for them to think about the long-term problem of climate change as it is for them to think about jobs or education or health care.

About The Project

Have you ever wondered when will the earth end? it will happen eventually, but when will it happen? Introducing... doom o'clock, a website that features a countdown that is predicted not based on black magic wizardry, but based on actual data using artificial intelegence and machine learning. We have used are moddel to calculate when CO2 levels will surpass 450 ppm, an amount scientents and determined will indicate irreversible climate damage. You might be asking, "cool, but what are the datas the countdown is based on?" we feed doom o'clock's data from the Climatic Research Unit at the University of East Anglia, ESRL/NOAA Global Monitoring Division, Godard Institute for Space Studies at NASA, SOLARIS-HEPPA project website, and from the ESRL/NOAA Physical Sciences Division so it can predict the outcome at a certain month of a certain year. You might also say "im scared, can we extend the countdown?" because the majority of the datas are caused by humans, then sure we can, doom 'o clock shows non-profit organizations and projects that are trying their hardest to make climate change cease to exist, so we got a bunch of these resources, jumbled them all up together, and put them in our resources tab so you can check out these major projects and help them out.
